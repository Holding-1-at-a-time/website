### A. NAP Consistency Rules
1. **Always use exact same format**:
   - Business Name: "One Detail At A Time" (not variations)
   - Phone: "(726) 207-1007" (with parentheses and hyphen)
   - Address: "11692 Bricken Circle, San Antonio, TX 78233"
   - Hours: Exact format as listed above

2. **Avoid these common mistakes**:
   - Don't abbreviate differently (St vs Street)
   - Don't change phone number format between listings
   - Don't use different business names (LLC vs no LLC)

### B. Citation Quality Tips
1. **Complete all available fields**:
   - Business description (150-200 words)
   - Service areas (San Antonio, TX and specific neighborhoods)
   - Business hours (exact format)
   - Categories (Auto Detailing, Car Wash, etc.)

2. **Add photos and descriptions**:
   - Before/after photos of detailing work
   - Business exterior/interior photos
   - Professional headshots if available

3. **Encourage reviews on these platforms**:
   - Google (primary)
   - Yelp
   - Facebook
   - TripAdvisor (if applicable)
